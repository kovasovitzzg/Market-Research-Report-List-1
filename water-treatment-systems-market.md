<p><p>The water treatment systems market encompasses several types, each serving distinct purposes. Filtration systems remove impurities and contaminants through physical barriers, while water softeners address hard water issues by replacing calcium and magnesium ions with sodium. Distillation systems purify water by boiling it and condensing the steam, effectively eliminating pathogens and solids. Disinfection methods, such as chlorination or UV treatment, ensure the water is safe for consumption. Other systems may include reverse osmosis and chemical treatments, catering to diverse purification needs.</p></p>

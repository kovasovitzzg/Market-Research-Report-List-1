<p><ul><li>Chain</li><li>Hinged</li><li>Solid Bar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Handcuffs are categorized into three main market types: chain, hinged, and solid bar. Chain handcuffs feature interconnected links, allowing flexibility for the wearer, often used by law enforcement for ease of movement. Hinged handcuffs provide a more restrictive fit with a hinge mechanism, limiting the wearer's movement for added security. Solid bar handcuffs offer robust control with a rigid design, making them difficult to escape from. Each type serves unique needs in security and restraint applications.</p></p>

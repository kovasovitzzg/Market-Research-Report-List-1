<p><p>Crocodile shear market types are primarily classified into Flywheel Drive and Hydraulic Drive systems. Flywheel Drive shears utilize a rotating flywheel to store kinetic energy, providing powerful cutting strokes efficiently. These are typically used for heavy-duty applications. In contrast, Hydraulic Drive shears rely on hydraulic systems to generate cutting force, offering precise control and versatility, making them suitable for a variety of materials. Both types cater to distinct operational needs in metal recycling and processing industries.</p></p>

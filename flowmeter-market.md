<p><strong>The Flowmeter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Type</li><li>Mechanical Type</li><li>Ultrasonic Type</li><li>Magnetic Type</li><li>Pressure Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flowmeter market encompasses several types, each suited for specific applications. Thermal flowmeters measure fluid flow by assessing temperature changes, ideal for gases. Mechanical flowmeters, such as gear and diaphragm types, utilize moving parts to quantify flow, commonly used in water and HVAC systems. Ultrasonic flowmeters employ sound waves to determine flow rates, suitable for various liquids. Magnetic flowmeters operate on electromagnetic principles, effective for conductive fluids. Pressure flowmeters gauge flow based on pressure differences, often used in industrial applications.</p></p>

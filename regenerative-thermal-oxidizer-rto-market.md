The market features two main types: Three-bed RTOs, which utilize three distinct chambers for efficient heat recovery and thermal oxidation, maximizing energy efficiency and minimizing emissions; and Rotary RTOs, which employ a rotating ceramic media to enhance heat recovery and treatment efficiency. Both types cater to industries requiring stringent air quality compliance, providing effective solutions for VOC reduction.</p></p>

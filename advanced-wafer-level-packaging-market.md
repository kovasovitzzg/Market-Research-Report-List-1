<p><strong>The Advanced Wafer Level Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Fan-Out Wafer-Level Packaging (FOWLP)"</li><li>"Fan-In Wafer-Level Packaging (FIWLP)"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Advanced Wafer Level Packaging market consists of two key types: Fan-Out Wafer-Level Packaging (FOWLP) and Fan-In Wafer-Level Packaging (FIWLP). FOWLP allows for a larger number of input/output (I/O) connections by extending the chip footprint beyond the wafer, enabling efficient heat dissipation and improved performance in compact spaces. In contrast, FIWLP retains a traditional chip size with integrated connections directly underneath, offering a space-efficient solution for standard applications. Both technologies enhance device miniaturization and performance in electronics.</p></p>

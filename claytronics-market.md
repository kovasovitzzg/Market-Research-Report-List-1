<p><p>Claytronics encompasses various market types based on different categories of "catoms" or programmable matter. Planer catoms work by creating flat surfaces for dynamic shapes, while electrostatic catoms utilize electric fields for movement and bonding. Giant helium catoms focus on larger scale applications, leveraging buoyancy for manipulation in three-dimensional spaces. Millimeter scale catoms are smaller components designed for intricate assembly and control, enabling complex structures. Together, these types advance the field of dynamic, programmable materials for diverse applications.</p></p>

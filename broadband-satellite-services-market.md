<p><p>Broadband satellite services are categorized into several frequency bands, each with unique characteristics. C Band offers extensive coverage and reliability, suitable for rural areas but with lower data speeds. Ku Band provides higher data rates and is commonly used for consumer and enterprise applications, though it is more affected by rain fade. Ka Band offers even higher speeds and capacity, making it ideal for high-demand applications like streaming. Other bands may include advancements or alternatives that cater to specific needs or regions.</p></p>

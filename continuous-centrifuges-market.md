<p><ul><li>Continuous Filtration Centrifuges</li><li>Continuous Sedimentation Centrifuges</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The continuous centrifuges market comprises two main types: continuous filtration centrifuges and continuous sedimentation centrifuges. Continuous filtration centrifuges efficiently separate solids from liquids using a filter medium, ideal for applications requiring consistent product quality, such as in food and pharmaceutical industries. In contrast, continuous sedimentation centrifuges utilize gravity or centrifugal force to separate materials based on density differences, commonly used in wastewater treatment and mineral processing. Both types enhance productivity, reduce processing times, and optimize resource utilization in various industries.</p></p>

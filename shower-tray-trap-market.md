<p><p>The shower tray trap market is categorized into two main types: shallow and deep traps. Shallow traps are designed for low-profile installations, making them ideal for modern, minimalist shower designs where space is limited. They typically handle lower water flow and are easier to install. In contrast, deep traps are suited for higher water flow scenarios, effectively preventing clogs and odors. They are often used in traditional showers with higher drainage needs, providing enhanced performance in maintaining hygiene.</p></p>

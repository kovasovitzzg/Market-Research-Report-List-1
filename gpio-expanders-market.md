<p><ul><li>Quasi-bidirectional GPIO</li><li>LED PWM Open-drain GPIO</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The GPIO expander market encompasses various types, including quasi-bidirectional GPIO, LED PWM, and open-drain GPIO. Quasi-bidirectional GPIO allows signal management in both directions, enhancing flexibility in device communication. LED PWM GPIO facilitates effective control of LED brightness through pulse width modulation, making it ideal for lighting applications. Open-drain GPIO enables multiple devices to share a single line for pull-up signal management, essential in scenarios requiring fault tolerance and bus sharing. Together, these expanders play crucial roles in modern electronic systems.</p></p>

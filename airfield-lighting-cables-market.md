<p><strong>The Airfield Lighting Cables Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Primary circuit</li><li>Secondary circuit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The airfield lighting cables market can be divided into two main types: primary circuits and secondary circuits. Primary circuit cables are responsible for transmitting high voltage power from the electricity source to the airfield lighting systems, ensuring robust illumination for runways and taxiways. Secondary circuit cables, on the other hand, operate at lower voltages and are used for control and signaling purposes, connecting the lighting fixtures and providing operational commands. Both types are essential for safe and effective airfield operations.</p></p>

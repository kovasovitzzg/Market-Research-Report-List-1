<p><ul><li>Aerosol BOV</li><li>Standard BOV</li><li>Non-spray/Low-pressure BOV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bag-on-valve (BOV) technology market is categorized into three types: Aerosol BOV, Standard BOV, and Non-spray/Low-pressure BOV. Aerosol BOV utilizes a propellant to create a fine mist, ideal for products requiring spray application, such as cosmetics or pharmaceuticals. Standard BOV emphasizes user-friendly dispensing without contamination, suitable for a variety of consumer goods. Non-spray/Low-pressure BOV is designed for thicker formulations, providing controlled dispensing for creams and gels while minimizing waste, making it popular in personal care and food industries.</p></p>

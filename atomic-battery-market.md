<p><ul><li>Thermal Conversion Type</li><li>Non-Thermal Conversion Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The atomic battery market is categorized into two main types: thermal conversion and non-thermal conversion. Thermal conversion types utilize heat generated from radioactive decay to produce electricity, effectively harnessing the energy from isotopes. In contrast, non-thermal conversion types directly convert radiation emitted by radioactive materials into electrical energy without intermediate heat transfer. These technologies are pivotal in applications like space exploration and remote sensors, focusing on efficiency, longevity, and reliability to meet the demands of various industries.</p></p>

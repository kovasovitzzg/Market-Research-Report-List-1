<p><strong>The Car Fleet Leasing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Close End Lease</li><li>Open End Lease</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car fleet leasing market consists primarily of two types: close-end leases and open-end leases. Close-end leases allow businesses to return the vehicle at the end of the term without any further financial obligations, as long as mileage and condition guidelines are met. In contrast, open-end leases require the lessee to cover any depreciation costs if the vehicle's value drops below a predetermined level, providing more flexibility but exposing the lessee to potential additional costs.</p></p>

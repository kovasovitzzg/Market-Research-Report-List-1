<p><ul><li>Loose Ball Bearings</li><li>Retainer Bearings</li><li>Sealed Cartridge Bearings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bicycle bearings market features three main types: loose ball bearings, retainer bearings, and sealed cartridge bearings. Loose ball bearings consist of individual balls that provide minimal friction but require regular maintenance. Retainer bearings use a cage to hold the balls, enhancing stability and ease of installation. Sealed cartridge bearings are pre-assembled and sealed against dirt and moisture, offering low maintenance and durability. Each type caters to different biking preferences and performance needs, influencing their popularity among cyclists.</p></p>

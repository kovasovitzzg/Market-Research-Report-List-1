<p><p>The mechanical steering gear market encompasses various types, including rotary, rack, and hydraulic systems. Rotary steering gears utilize a circular motion mechanism for precise control, making them suitable for compact vehicles. Rack steering gears use a linear motion to convert rotational input into lateral movement, common in many modern cars for responsiveness. Hydraulic steering gears employ fluid pressure to enhance steering ease and control, often found in larger vehicles due to their efficiency and strength in handling heavy loads.</p></p>

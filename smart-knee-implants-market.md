<p><p>The smart knee implants market is primarily divided into two types: total knee implants and partial knee implants. Total knee implants replace the entirety of the knee joint, providing comprehensive pain relief and improved function for patients with severe osteoarthritis or injury. In contrast, partial knee implants target only the damaged section of the knee, preserving healthy bone and tissue. This tailored approach allows for a less invasive procedure, quicker recovery, and often better mobility for patients with localized knee problems.</p></p>

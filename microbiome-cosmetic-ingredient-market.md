<p><p>The microbiome cosmetic ingredient market comprises three primary types: probiotics, prebiotics, and postbiotics. Probiotics are live beneficial bacteria that promote skin health by balancing the microbiome and enhancing the skin barrier. Prebiotics serve as food for these beneficial microorganisms, fostering a healthy microbiome environment. Postbiotics are byproducts of probiotic fermentation, offering skin-soothing and anti-inflammatory properties. Together, these ingredients support skin resilience, hydration, and overall appearance, driving trends in the natural and wellness-focused beauty industry.</p></p>

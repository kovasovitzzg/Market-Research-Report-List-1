<p><ul><li>Abrasive Type</li><li>Normal Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Hard CMP pads market is primarily divided into two types: abrasive and normal. Abrasive type pads are designed for aggressive material removal, utilizing granular materials to enhance polishing efficiency for harder substrates like silicon wafers. In contrast, normal type pads are suited for smoother finishes and are used in applications requiring less aggressive polishing action. Both types play essential roles in semiconductor manufacturing, affecting surface quality and yield in the production of microelectronics.</p></p>

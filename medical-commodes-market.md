<p><p>Medical commodes are specialized toilet-like devices designed to assist individuals with mobility issues or disabilities in maintaining personal hygiene. Additionally, the shift towards home healthcare services, coupled with the rising number of surgical procedures, is expected to further boost the market. Recent trends indicate a growing interest in eco-friendly materials and designs that enhance user comfort, ultimately supporting a more dignified experience for individuals requiring assistance with toileting.

<p><p>The swimming pool cleaning machines market comprises three primary types: robotic pool cleaners, suction pool cleaners, and pressure pool cleaners. Robotic pool cleaners operate independently, using advanced navigation systems to efficiently scrub and vacuum debris. Suction pool cleaners connect to the pool's filtration system, utilizing the suction to collect dirt and leaves from the floor. Pressure pool cleaners, on the other hand, rely on water pressure from the pool's return line to propel themselves, effectively dislodging and trapping debris. Each type offers distinct cleaning solutions for various pool maintenance needs.</p></p>

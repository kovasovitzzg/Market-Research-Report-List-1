<p><p>The portable electric pressure washer market is mainly divided into two types: corded and cordless. Corded models are powered directly via an electrical outlet, offering consistent power and performance, making them ideal for prolonged use. They typically have higher pressure ratings but may limit mobility due to the need to stay within reach of a power source. In contrast, cordless pressure washers operate on rechargeable batteries, providing enhanced portability and convenience for users who need flexibility in accessing various cleaning areas without cords.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Coulomb Type</li><li>Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ceramic electrostatic chucks (ESCs) are essential in semiconductor manufacturing for wafer handling. The Coulomb type uses electrostatic forces generated through an electric field to hold wafers securely, ensuring accurate positioning during processes. In contrast, the Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) type leverages induced charge, creating a more uniform pressure distribution across the wafer surface. Both types enhance efficiency and precision in semiconductor equipment, catering to specific application needs and improving overall yield in manufacturing processes.</p></p>

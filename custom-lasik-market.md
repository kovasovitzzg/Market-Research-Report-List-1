<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Custom LASIK Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wavefront Guided LASIK</li><li>Wavefront Optimized LASIK</li><li>Topography Guided LASIK</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Custom LASIK market includes three primary types: </p><p>1. **Wavefront Guided LASIK** utilizes detailed wavefront analysis to create a tailored treatment plan, correcting higher-order aberrations for enhanced vision quality.</p><p>  </p><p>2. **Wavefront Optimized LASIK** aims to maintain the cornea's natural shape while minimizing potential side effects, focusing on standard refractive errors.</p><p>  </p><p>3. **Topography Guided LASIK** combines corneal mapping with wavefront data to address irregularities, offering precise treatment for astigmatism and other irregularities. Each type enhances personalized vision correction efficacy.</p></p>

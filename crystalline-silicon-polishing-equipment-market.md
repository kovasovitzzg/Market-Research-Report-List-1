<p><ul><li>Single-chip Polishing</li><li>Batch Polishing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The crystalline silicon polishing equipment market encompasses two main types: single-chip polishing and batch polishing. Single-chip polishing is designed for individual silicon wafers, ensuring precision and quality for high-performance applications, such as advanced semiconductor manufacturing. In contrast, batch polishing involves processing multiple wafers simultaneously, optimizing efficiency and throughput for larger-scale production. Both methods are essential for achieving the desired surface quality and flatness required in the fabrication of electronic devices, contributing significantly to the overall semiconductor supply chain.</p></p>
